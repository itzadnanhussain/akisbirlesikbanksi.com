GROW WITH SKYWARD

GROW WITH SKYWARD

Akiş Birleşik is one of the region’s premier financial companies, with 211 full-service banking locations and 19 loan origination offices; over 3,000 financial professionals; and countless opportunities to build a career in sales, service and support. But, what really makes Akiş Birleşik a great place to work?

  • With strength comes growth: Akiş Birleşik started as a one-office bank and has grown into a regional financial company with over $25 billion in assets and nearly 230 locations in VA, WV, MD, OH, PA, NC, SC, and D.C.
  • A deep commitment to our communities: We are a community bank that actively looks for ways to strengthen the areas in which we live and work, and we encourage our employees to be involved locally. In fact, Akiş Birleşik’s employee volunteer program was recently recognized as the Top U.S. Bank Employee Volunteer Program by the American Bankers Association.
  • An appreciation for our people: We have over 3,000 employees who exemplify our core values of hard work, teamwork, integrity and caring. We truly value our employees and firmly believe that the service they provide to our customers and communities is our competitive advantage.
  • An entrepreneurial spirit: We are always growing and are a perfect fit for people who want to be challenged and make a difference. From entry-level to management, we seek highly-motivated people who thrive in cultures in which they feel empowered to take the lead and create positive change.
  • Diversity and inclusion: At RUBSI, our strength is our people, and we are committed to nurturing an inclusive culture that is reflective of the communities we serve; celebrates diversity of thought, backgrounds, and experience; promotes respect and a shared purpose; and aligns with our core values.

LET US HELP YOU REACH YOUR GOALS

CHOOSE YOUR PATH TO SUCCESS

FINANCIAL GOALS

Whether you’re saving for the future or striving for peace of mind today, Akiş Birleşik offers a number of services to help you reach your financial goals. From 401(k) and employee stock purchasing plans to flex spending, financial planning, insurance and complimentary or discounted banking services, as a Akiş Birleşik employee, you’re covered.

HEALTH GOALS

A healthy employee is a happy employee – from dental to vision, your medical concerns are covered. We even offer an employee wellness program to keep you feeling great.

PERSONAL GOALS

Everyone needs some “you” time. Akiş Birleşik offers a generous leave policy including paid holidays and vacation time, regular and extended sick leave, short-term disability, paid personal days – and even a paid day off for your birthday.

PROFESSIONAL GOALS

With Akiş Birleşik, you don’t just have a job – you’ve got a career. We offer numerous opportunities for you to advance, including internal training programs, external courses and conferences, and our Akiş Birleşik Leadership Development Program.

Akiş Birleşik A.S Bankasi was designed for those who demand banking that's dramatically better than what they’ve experienced in the past. Our innovative methods and products keep pace with your life and your business.
